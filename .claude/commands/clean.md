Clean and format Ertugrul learning materials. Usage:

/clean [season] [episode_start] [episode_end] [source_lang] [target_lang]

Examples:
- /clean 1 1 1 en tr (Clean episode 1 season 1 Turkish materials)
- /clean 1 1 5 en ar (Clean episodes 1-5 season 1 Arabic materials)

This command will:
1. Clean and format subtitle files
2. Remove unnecessary whitespace and formatting
3. Standardize timing information
4. Validate subtitle format consistency
5. Update language dictionaries with cleaned terms
6. Generate final learning-ready materials

The cleaning process ensures optimal quality for language learning by removing artifacts and standardizing formats.
